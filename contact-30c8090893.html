Frequently Asked Questions

What is your minimum order quantity (MOQ)?

Our MOQ varies by product. For most standard items, the MOQ is 10 pieces. Custom orders may have different requirements. Contact us for specific product details.

Do you offer custom designs and OEM services?

Yes! We specialize in custom designs and OEM services. Our team can work with your specifications to create unique crystal products for your brand.

What are your payment terms?

We accept various payment methods including T/T (Wire Transfer), PayPal, and Western Union. Typical payment terms are 30% deposit and 70% before shipment.

How long is your production and delivery time?

Production time varies based on order quantity and complexity. Standard orders typically take 2-4 weeks. Expedited production is available for urgent orders.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship worldwide from Yiwu, China. We work with reliable international shipping companies to ensure safe and timely delivery to your location.

Can I request product samples?

Yes, samples are available for most products. Sample fees may apply but will be deducted from your bulk order. Contact us for sample availability and pricing.
